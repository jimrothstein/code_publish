Let x $\in [0,1]$
And consider this every day function:

$$
f=\begin{cases}
	0&	\text{if x rational}, \\
	1&	\text{if x irrational}.
	\end{cases}
$$


Compute the area under the curve.  Show your work.


$$
\int _{a}^{b}f(x)dx=\int _{a}^{c}f(x)dx+\int _{c}^{b}f(x)dx
$$
